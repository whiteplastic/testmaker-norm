###Register user
For some test, it is neccessary to register to be able to take them. Please enter a username and a 6 or more digit password, which you can keep im mind easily. Furthermore you are asked to enter your full name (Prename, Surname) and your email address. Finally, you can choose a language for the test menue (English, German). As soon as you are logged in you can still change all details by choosing the option "user details". 

When you press the button register, you will be sent an email with a confirmation link to the address you entered. You are asked to use the link during the following 7 days, otherwise the registration will be deleted.
